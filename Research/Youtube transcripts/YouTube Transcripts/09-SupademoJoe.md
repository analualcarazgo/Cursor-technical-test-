# SupademoJoe: Product construction/demo strategy
**Source:** How I Validate My SaaS Ideas FAST (Step-by-Step) (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lcDW9F5h23I)
**Key Takeaways:** Here Joseph Lee takes us through a 3 step framework for validating SaaS ideas. 
* *Focus on Behavioral Discovery:* Instead of asking potential customers what they would do, conduct structured interviews to uncover what they actually have done to solve their problems, such as using spreadsheets, manual labor, or existing workarounds.
* *Prioritize Evidence of Commitment:* Validate your SaaS idea by testing if users are willing to invest real value—such as time, money, or effort—into your solution before you build the final product. Tools like manual service offers, design partner agreements, or paid betas are excellent for this.
* *Identify the "Why Now":* Before going all-in, ensure you can clearly articulate why this problem hasn't been solved until now. Success requires a shift in the market, new buyer behavior, or new technology that makes your solution viable today where it wasn't before.
* *Avoid the Sunk Cost Trap:* Do not let emotional or temporal investment in a project blind you to weak market signals. If customers require constant begging or follow-ups to engage, be prepared to walk away and pivot rather than continuing to build in a dead-end market.
