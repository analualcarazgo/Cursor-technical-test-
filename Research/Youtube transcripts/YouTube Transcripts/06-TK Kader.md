# TK Kader: Product and persuasion
**Source:** [Product Demos That Sell: 7 Elements of Insanely Persuasive Sales Demos] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AxNu7oeVG0M)
**Key Takeaways:** Here explains through his experience what are the top product demos that sell and what topics to keep in mind for your business. 
* **Strategic Narrative**: Successful product demos avoid feature-dumping and instead tell a cohesive story connecting market problems to product transformation.
* **The SaaS Venn Diagram**: Effective sales are built at the intersection of three pillars: Target Market, Product Solution, and Go-to-Market execution.
* **Demo Flow Architecture**:
    * **Action Shot**: Initiates the demo immediately within the core product loop—the daily task the user performs—to drive instant engagement.
    * **No Hands Mode**: Showcases automated workflows, AI capabilities, and background integrations that provide value without requiring manual user effort.
    * **Manager's View**: Shifts the focus to decision-makers, demonstrating high-level analytics, team visibility, and administrative controls.
* **ROI & Risk Positioning**:
    * Demos must clearly articulate business ROI: generating revenue, saving costs, or reducing organizational risk.
    * Persuasion is bolstered by explicitly highlighting the business risks of maintaining the status quo.
* **Transformation Focus**: The ultimate goal is selling a transformative change in the customer's business or life, rather than just selling software features.
