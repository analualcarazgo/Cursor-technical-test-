# 01-Seth Godin: Marketing Philosophy & Permission Marketing
**Source:** [How to build a Remarkable Brand in the age of AI] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zFHzTy7XLbM)
**Key Takeaways:** In this video they talk about how telling a story resonates with different audiences. The age of AI in marketing and why differentiation is the key to build a brand.
* *Trust over tech:* In a world flooded with AI generated content, trust is the only thing that actually makes you stand out. 
* *Differentiation:* Don't try to please everyone. Being "remarkable" kust means being important to a specific group of people. 
* *The human touch:* AI is great for speed, but it will never replace the empathy or the real purpose behind a brand.
* *Smallest viable audience:* It’s way better to focus on building a real community that gets your vision than to chase the masses.
**Relevance:**
Godin reminds us that even in B2B, we're still selling human to human. For a new project, this means we can't just fall into the trap of using AI for everything. We need to tell real stories that connect with people.
