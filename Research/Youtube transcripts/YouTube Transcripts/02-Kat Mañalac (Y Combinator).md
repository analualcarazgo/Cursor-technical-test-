# 02-Kat Mañalac: Startup launch and traction.
**Source:** [The best way to launch a stratup] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u36A-YTxiOw&t=195s)
**Key Takeaways:** In this video, she discusses why clarity in your value proposition is non-negotiable and how focusing on real user pain points is the only way to build something that lasts.
* *Clarity:* When explaining your startup, keep it simple. If you can't explain it in one sentence, you don't understand your own value proposition yet.
* *Focus on the user:* Don't get distracted by vanity metrics. Talk to your actual users, listen to their specific pains, and build features that solve those specific problems.
* *Speed of iteration:* It's better to launch something small and get feedback immediately than to spend months building a perfect product that nobody wants.
* *The "Why":* Investors don't just care about the product; they care about why you are the right person to build it and why this needs to exist right now.
**Relevance:** If you're recent to B2B SaaS or starting your business, Kat's approach is essential for your customer or user experience because she treats building a product as a process of empathy and direct communication, not just coding features.
