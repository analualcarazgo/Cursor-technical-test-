### RESEARCH SOURCES: B2B SAAS YOUTUBE CONTENT STRATEGY
This repository contains a curated analysis of high impact content strategies for B2B SaaS. To ensure the quality of insights, a systematic audit of expert-led content on Youtube was performed to synthesize methodologies in product-led growth, retention and content scalability. 
**The following experts have been identified as key references for their contribution to the field:**
1. Seth Godin: Marketing philosophy and permission marketing. 
2. Kat Manalac (Y Combinator): Startup growth and early stage strategy. 
3. David Lieb (Y Combinator): Product retention and user centric experience. 
4. Rob Walling: Conversion and UX. 
5. Wes Bush: Product Led Growth.
6. TK Kader: B2B Sales. 
7. HubSpot Marketing: Distribution and SEO. 
8. Operations Kevin: Execution. How he builds a SaaS startup. 
9. SupademoJoe: Product construction/demo strategy. SaaS idea validation.
10. Denis Shatalin: B2B SaaS GTM strategy for scaling startups.
