# 05-Wes Bush (Product Led): Product Led Growth.
**Source:** [Product Led Growth Framework Masterclass] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jvOZMQYjYYY)
**Key Takeaways:** Wes Bush talks about haw you can use product-led growth to accelerathe the growth of a business.
* *Eliminating the Value Gap (Time-To-Value):* The core goal is to minimize the time it takes for a user to experience the product's value. The speaker advises auditing the onboarding process to determine which steps can be eliminated, delayed, or simplified to accelerate this journey.
* *The Bowling Alley Framework:* This framework uses a metaphor to guide users toward success. It involves establishing a "straight-line" onboarding experience (the mission-critical steps) and adding "bumpers" to prevent users from falling into the "gutter" or abandoning the product before they see its value.
* *Utilizing Product Bumpers:* These are in-product elements designed to support the user's progress. Key examples include product tours, checklists, educational empty states, and tooltips, which help guide users toward the actions that define their success.
* *Signals:* Drawing inspiration from Toyota’s manufacturing efficiency, this strategy focuses on reacting to user behavior. If a user fails to complete a key step, the system triggers automated, timely messages (via email or other channels) to bring them back to the product and help them reach the desired outcome.
