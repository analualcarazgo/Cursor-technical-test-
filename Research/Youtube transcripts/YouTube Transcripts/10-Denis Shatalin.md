# Denis Shatalin: B2B Saas Strategy. 
**Source:** [The only B2B SaaS GTM strategy I use after scaling 150 startups] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m0LcuCjx0qA)
**Key Takeaways:** Denis presents the PACE framework (Pain, Audience, Channel and Expansion) to help B2B SaaS stratups scale rapidly. 
* *Solve Urgent Pain:* Prioritize problems that are expensive and vital to the organization, which allows for higher pricing and faster conversion.
* *Target High-Value Audiences:* Focus on winning companies with existing software budgets and high 'cost of inaction' rather than trying to fix struggling businesses.
* *Prioritize Predictable Channels:* Shift away from manual outbound or content grinds toward paid traffic (Meta/Google Ads) for scalable, systematic growth.
* *Maximize Expansion:* Prioritize upsells and enterprise rollouts, aiming to get customers to repay their acquisition cost quickly to double the company every month.
